About Me

Cesar Rodriguez is a passionate artist, Ui and Ux engineer and a makeup artist with a lot of goals. He has an enthusiastic view of the world. Cesar is always ready to learn and discover anything and everything. He is a student studying Multimedia Programming (MMP). Altho these two worlds rarely meet. Cesar has found a way to mesh them and renovate the world with a fresh sense of colors, themes, and a bit of fashion to any job and field he falls upon. He loves every second of his fashion-driven nerdy life.
